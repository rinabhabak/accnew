BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT MANAGER - AUTOMOTIVE

Responsible for growth and maintenance of business-to-business sales with Automotive Accounts.

  • Increase OEM sales volume through a variety of account activities, exceptional customer support and the utilization of technical field knowledge.
  • Create and Close new business opportunities within defined target markets as we all share expansion within current customer base.

ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:

Manages sales to Original Equipment Manufactures (OEMs):

  • Develop and grow selling relationships with existing major Tier 1 and Tier 2 automotive accounts nationwide.
  • Define and develop new OEM opportunities in accordance with the Accuride Target Market Focus and Sales Tool Kit.
    • Define target potentials as defined by the pre-determined target markets and score target account opportunities.
    • Develop milestones to win opportunities uncovered through the relating and discovery stages of the sales cycle.
  • Interact directly with engineering teams to define and clearly communicate customer requirements in accordance with PDM protocol.
  • Develop, maintain and continually update market intelligence on major customers and competitors position in the region.
  • Responsible for preparing sales forecast for territory and related budgeting for sales expenses for submittal to Director of Sales.

ESSENTIAL JOB REQUIREMENTS:

KNOWLEDGE REQUIRED:

  • An undergraduate degree in business/engineering or equivalent technical sales experience.
  • At least five years of experience in selling a technical/engineered product, where solution selling plays a central theme.
  • Demonstrated experience with consultative selling techniques (Example â€“ Wilson Counselor Sales Program)
  • Demonstrated experience and successes in direct solution selling to major OEM accounts.
  • Experienced analysis of and familiarity with target markets, SAM, TAM, Scored Targets etc.

SKILLS AND ABILITIES REQUIRED:

  • Excellent oral and written communications skills; highly developed interpersonal and presentation skills capable of dealing with a variety of cultures and backgrounds.
  • Demonstrated record of accomplishment in dealing with senior ranking officials.
  • Strong negotiation and organizational skills; proficiency with standard office PC and software such as Word, Excel and PowerPoint.

PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS:

  • Travel requirement of up to 60% with occasional travel to the corporate office in Santa Fe Springs, California.
